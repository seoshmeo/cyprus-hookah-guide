Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Cyprus Hookah Guide ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website.

Information We Do Not Collect

We take a privacy-first approach. Our website:

  • Does not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes
  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not use analytics services like Google Analytics
  • Does not track your browsing behavior
  • Does not share any data with third parties
  • Does not require account registration

Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage solely to remember your age verification confirmation. This data:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is never transmitted to our servers
  • Can be cleared by clearing your browser data
  • Contains no personal information

External Links

Our website contains links to external websites (such as Google Maps for venue locations). These third-party sites have their own privacy policies, and we have no control over their content or practices.

Third-Party Images

Some images on our website may be loaded from third-party services. When this occurs, those services may receive your IP address. We strive to minimize such external requests.

Hosting

Our website is hosted on Vercel. While we do not collect data, Vercel may collect standard server logs including IP addresses for security and performance purposes. Please refer to Vercel's Privacy Policy for more information.

Age Restriction

This website contains information about tobacco-related products and is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. By using this website, you confirm that you meet this age requirement.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website.

Your Rights Under GDPR

As we do not collect personal data, most GDPR rights regarding data access, rectification, or deletion do not apply. However, if you have any concerns about your privacy, please feel free to contact us.
