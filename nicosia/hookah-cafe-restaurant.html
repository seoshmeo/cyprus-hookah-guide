About

Hookah Cafe Restaurant is one of the most popular shisha lounges in South Nicosia. Known for its wide tobacco selection and relaxed Middle Eastern atmosphere, this venue has become a go-to destination for hookah enthusiasts in the capital.

The late-night hours make it perfect for those looking to enjoy quality shisha after other venues close. The authentic atmosphere and extensive menu have earned it a loyal following among locals and visitors alike.
