About

Hookah Club Lounge is a popular shisha destination in Larnaca, offering a comfortable lounge experience with quality hookah and food. The venue provides a welcoming atmosphere for both casual visitors and hookah enthusiasts.

With convenient hours from noon to midnight every day, Hookah Club Lounge is perfect for an afternoon session, evening relaxation, or a late-night smoke with friends.
