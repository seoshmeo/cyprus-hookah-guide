About

River Rock Cocktail Bar & Shisha Lounge offers a stylish setting for those seeking creative cocktails and a relaxed hookah experience in Ayia Napa. The venue combines sophisticated drinks with quality shisha in a comfortable atmosphere.

Perfect for a more laid-back evening compared to the high-energy clubs, River Rock provides a refined space to enjoy good company, excellent drinks, and smooth shisha.
