About

Cafe Central is the signature lounge at the prestigious Napa Plaza Hotel, offering guests a premium resort experience complete with a dedicated shisha menu. The venue provides a relaxed, upscale atmosphere perfect for hotel guests and visitors alike.

Enjoy hookah in a beautiful poolside setting with attentive service and quality amenities. The lounge is ideal for those seeking a more refined, resort-style shisha experience away from the bustling nightlife areas.
