About Hookah Culture in Ayia Napa

Ayia Napa, known as the party capital of Cyprus, offers a diverse hookah scene that caters to every preference. From family-run oriental lounges serving the best shisha on the island to glamorous nightclubs where DJs spin until dawn, the variety is unmatched.

The resort town's hookah establishments range from cozy afternoon spots perfect for relaxation to high-energy late-night venues that keep the party going. Many places combine quality shisha with excellent food, creative cocktails, and live entertainment.

Whether you're looking for a peaceful sunset session with sea views, a lively pub with live music, or a sophisticated lounge experience, Ayia Napa delivers. The season runs from April to October, but several venues operate year-round for the dedicated hookah enthusiast.
