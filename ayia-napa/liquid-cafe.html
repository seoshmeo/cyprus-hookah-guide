About

Liquid Cafe N Bar is a multi-level cafe, bar, and restaurant that serves as a social hub near Town Square in Ayia Napa. With bright, beach-style decor and multiple seating terraces, it's lively from day through night.

The venue offers a full food and drinks menu throughout the day, with shisha available in the evenings. Perfect for those looking for an all-day destination that transitions from casual cafe to vibrant nightlife spot.
