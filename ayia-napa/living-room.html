About

Living Room is widely regarded as "the best lounge bar in Ayia Napa" by locals and visitors alike. This contemporary restaurant-cafe-lounge offers a versatile experience with great ambiance, good music, and quality shisha in the evenings.

The venue features a lovely, modern interior with good lighting and even heaters for chilly nights. Live DJs create the perfect atmosphere while guests enjoy excellent food, cocktails, and hookah. The service consistently receives praise for being top-notch.
