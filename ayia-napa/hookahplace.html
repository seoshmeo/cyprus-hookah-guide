About

HookahPlace Ayia Napa is part of the world's biggest shisha lounge chain, bringing international hookah standards to Cyprus. Located near the Shrine of Panagia, this franchise offers high-quality shisha services in a modern, upscale environment.

The decor is modern and minimalist, creating a sophisticated lounge atmosphere with cushy sofas and chic design elements. Perfect for those who appreciate consistent quality and a contemporary vibe.
