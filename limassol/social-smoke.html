About

Social Smoke Lounge is Limassol's modern hotspot for those seeking a complete nightlife experience. Located near the seafront, this venue combines premium hookah with creative cocktails and regular DJ nights.

The vibrant atmosphere and prime location make it perfect for starting or ending a night out. Whether you're looking to dance, socialize, or simply enjoy quality shisha with sea breezes, Social Smoke delivers.
