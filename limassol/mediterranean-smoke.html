About

Mediterranean Smoke offers the ultimate beachfront hookah experience on Limassol's beloved Molos Promenade. With the sound of waves as your backdrop and the Mediterranean breeze carrying your smoke away, this is the perfect spot for a relaxed shisha session.

The venue features comfortable outdoor seating with direct views of the sea. It's particularly magical during sunset when the sky turns golden and the atmosphere becomes truly enchanting. Their cocktail menu complements the shisha perfectly.
