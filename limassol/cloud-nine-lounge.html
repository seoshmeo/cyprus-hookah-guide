About

Cloud Nine Lounge is Limassol's premier hookah destination, offering an unparalleled shisha experience at the stunning Limassol Marina. With breathtaking views of luxury yachts and the Mediterranean Sea, this upscale venue combines world-class service with an extensive selection of premium tobaccos.

The lounge features both indoor and outdoor seating, with comfortable lounge furniture and an ambient atmosphere that's perfect for evening relaxation. Their expert hookah masters prepare each shisha with precision, ensuring optimal flavor and smoke quality.
