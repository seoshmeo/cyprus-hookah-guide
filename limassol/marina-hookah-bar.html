About

Marina Hookah Bar offers the marina experience without the premium price tag. Located on the West Pier of Limassol Marina, this casual spot has become a favorite among locals who want to enjoy quality shisha while watching the boats come and go.

The atmosphere is laid-back and welcoming, with comfortable outdoor seating and friendly staff who remember their regulars. Their daily happy hour makes it an affordable option for those who want marina views without breaking the bank.
