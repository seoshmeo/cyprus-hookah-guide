About

Three Monkeys Shisha Bar offers a relaxed lounge atmosphere that has made it a favorite among Limassol locals. The venue combines affordable pricing with friendly service and quality hookah.

Unlike the more upscale venues in town, Three Monkeys provides a laid-back environment where you can enjoy great shisha without breaking the bank. The welcoming atmosphere and regular crowd create a genuine neighborhood hangout vibe.
