About

Seaside Shisha Club embodies the perfect Cyprus summer experience. Located directly on the beach in Germasogeia, this vibrant venue offers the unique pleasure of smoking hookah with your feet practically in the sand.

As the sun sets over the Mediterranean, the atmosphere transforms with ambient lighting and chill music. On weekends, local DJs spin laid-back beats creating an unforgettable beach party vibe. It's the go-to spot for young professionals and tourists looking for the ultimate summer hookah experience.
