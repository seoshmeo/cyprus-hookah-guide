About

Blue Wave Shisha proves that great hookah doesn't have to break the bank. Located in Limassol's popular tourist area near several major hotels, this laid-back spot has become a favorite among budget-conscious travelers and locals alike.

The venue features a pleasant outdoor terrace overlooking a nearby pool area. The atmosphere is casual and welcoming, perfect for an afternoon session or early evening relaxation. Staff are friendly and always happy to recommend flavors for newcomers.
