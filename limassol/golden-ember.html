About

Golden Ember represents the pinnacle of hookah culture in Cyprus. This exclusive establishment caters to discerning guests who appreciate the finer things in life. From the moment you arrive with complimentary valet service, you know you're in for a special experience.

The venue features opulent interiors with gold accents, plush velvet seating, and crystal chandeliers. Private rooms are available for those seeking complete discretion and exclusivity. Their hookah masters are among the best in the country, trained in traditional techniques while embracing modern innovations.
