About

Royal Tobacco House is Limassol's premier destination for the refined tobacco connoisseur. This sophisticated establishment combines the pleasures of premium hookah with an extensive cigar collection and one of the city's finest whisky selections.

The venue features classic British club-style interiors with leather Chesterfield sofas, dark wood paneling, and ambient lighting. It's the perfect setting for business discussions, celebrating special occasions, or simply enjoying an evening of refined pleasures with like-minded individuals.
