About

Shisha House Limassol is a traditional-style venue that stays true to the roots of hookah culture. Offering classic tobacco flavors alongside aromatic tea, this place provides an authentic, laid-back shisha experience.

For those seeking a genuine hookah atmosphere away from the trendy bar scene, Shisha House delivers. The focus on traditional preparation and classic flavors makes it a go-to for purists who appreciate the art of shisha.
