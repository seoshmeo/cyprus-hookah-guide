About

Oriental Nights brings the magic of the Middle East to Limassol. This unique venue combines exceptional hookah service with authentic Arabic cuisine and captivating entertainment, including live belly dancing performances on weekends.

The interior is lavishly decorated with Moroccan lamps, Persian carpets, and comfortable floor seating alongside traditional tables. It's the perfect venue for group celebrations, birthday parties, or simply an immersive cultural experience.
