About

Rockafellas Cocktail Bar & Shisha is one of the most popular nightlife destinations in Protaras. This vibrant venue combines premium cocktails, energetic DJ music, and quality shisha to create an unforgettable party atmosphere.

Very lively during the summer season, Rockafellas is particularly crowded in the evenings when tourists and locals gather to enjoy the electric atmosphere. It's the ideal choice for those seeking a party vibe and a social hookah experience in the heart of Protaras.
