About

Hippocampus Lounge Bar is a beautiful seaside venue offering cocktails, stunning sunset views, and occasional shisha nights. Located right by the water, this lounge bar combines premium drinks with breathtaking Mediterranean sea views.

Best visited in the early evening when the atmosphere is at its peak and the sunset creates a magical ambiance over the sea. The combination of quality cocktails, sea views, and shisha makes Hippocampus a memorable destination for special evenings in Protaras.
