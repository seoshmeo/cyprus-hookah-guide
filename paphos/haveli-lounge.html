About

Haveli Lounge is a casual and welcoming spot located close to Paphos old town. The venue offers quality shisha alongside light snacks, making it perfect for a relaxed late-evening session.

The informal atmosphere attracts a laid-back crowd looking for a comfortable place to enjoy hookah without the pretense of fancier venues. Its convenient location near the old town makes it an easy choice after exploring the historic area.
