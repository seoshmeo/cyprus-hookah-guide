About

Seven St. Georges Tavern offers a unique cultural experience that sets it apart from traditional hookah lounges. This venue combines the best of Cypriot hospitality with occasional shisha evenings, creating memorable experiences with local wine, authentic meze, and live music.

Perfect for those seeking more than just hookah, Seven St. Georges provides a complete cultural evening where shisha complements rather than dominates. The live music and traditional Cypriot atmosphere make it a must-visit for travelers seeking authentic local experiences.
