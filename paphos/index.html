About Hookah Culture in Paphos

Paphos, a UNESCO World Heritage city on Cyprus's southwest coast, offers a unique blend of ancient history and modern leisure. The hookah scene here caters to both tourists exploring the famous harbour and locals seeking relaxed evening entertainment.

From traditional Middle Eastern lounges serving authentic Arabic tea to trendy bars combining cocktails with premium shisha, Paphos has venues for every taste. Many spots are located near the harbour and old town, perfect for enjoying hookah with views of the Mediterranean.
