About

Arabica Shisha Lounge brings authentic Middle Eastern atmosphere to Paphos. This traditional lounge offers classic tobacco flavors alongside genuine Arabic tea, creating the perfect setting for a calm and relaxing evening.

The venue focuses on preserving the traditional hookah experience, making it ideal for those who appreciate authentic preparation methods and classic flavors. The peaceful ambiance and quality service have made it a favorite among hookah purists.
